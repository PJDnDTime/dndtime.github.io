<h2>Our Content</h2>
<p>We stream all sorts of D&D-related content throughout the week on <a href="https://twitch.tv/dndtime">Twitch.tv</a>:</p>
<p>Wednesdays at 6:30 PM PT is <strong><u>DnDTime Talks</strong></u>, a talkshow where Pete and Jeremy discuss all things D&D! We cover topics ranging from book reviews, to dungeon master advice and the show normally lasts about an hour.</p>
<p>Fridays at 6:00 PM PT is <strong><u>Pete & Jeremy's DnDTime</strong></u>, three one-shot adventures starring our viewers; Come play some D&D with we! Each adventure usually run no longer than one and a half hours and follow no particular storyline; Each adventure is completely self-contained.</p>
<p>Twice a month, Saturdays at 1:00 PM PT is <strong><u>DnDTime Behind the Screen</strong></u>, learn to prepare fun and simple one-shot adventures in Roll20! The show normally lasts about two hours as Jeremy walks through converting 4th Edition <a href="http://www.dmsguild.com/product/110211/">Dungeon Delve</a> adventures for 5th Edition D&D, and setting up the adventures in Roll20.</p>
<p>Twice a month, Sundays at 6:00 PM PT is <strong><u>DnDTime Delves</strong></u>, good old-fashioned one-shot dungeon crawl adventures! Jeremy runs a cast of four characters through the adventures prepped in <strong><u>DnDTime Behind the Screen</strong></u></p>. The game lasts between three and four hours and each game is, more or less, self-contained.</p>
<p>Twice a month, Sundays at 6:00 PM PT is <strong><u>DnDTime Brews</strong></u>, creating high quality homebrew for <strong><u>Pete & Jeremy's DnDTime</strong></u>! The show runs for between two and three hours as Pete and Jeremy hang out with the twitch chat and brew up new character options, such as race options, subclasses, or spells.
