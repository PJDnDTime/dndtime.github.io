<h2>Player Queue</h2>
<p>Register to play in Friday's episode of <strong>Pete & Jeremy's DnDTime</strong>. Sign-ups are available from Sunday through Wednesday, and Friday's players are announced on Thursday on our <a href="#/discord">discord server</a>. Game 1 each week is reserved to prioritize new players, so don't be afraid to sign up if you've never played with us before! For Games 2 and 3, players are selected based on their <strong>points</strong>. Points are earned by watching <a href="https://twitch.tv/dndtime" target="_blank">our stream</a>. Each cumulative hour watched earns one point. Every time a player participates in <strong>Pete & Jeremy's DnDTime</strong>, their point total is halved. Any attempts to abuse our point system will result in a ban from participation in <strong>Pete & Jeremy's DnDTime</strong>.</p>
