Player Queue

Sign up to play in this Friday's episode of Pete & Jeremy's DnDTime. We announce the players scheduled to play each week on Thursday on our Discord Server. We do our best to get new players in the first week that they sign up, so don't be afraid to join in if you've never played with us before! Players are selected to play each week in descending order of total accumulated DnDTime. You earn DnDTime automatically by watching Pete & Jeremy's DnDTime on Twitch.TV! Players who have played in back-to-back weeks have reduced priority regardless of their DnDTime to give everyone a more even chance to play with us. Whenever you play in our show, your currently accrued DnDTime total is halved.

     Sign-ups reset every week after we announce that week's players and are open from Friday through Wednesday. Don't forget to sign up each week that you want to play!
