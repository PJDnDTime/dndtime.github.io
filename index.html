Welcome to DnDTime!

DnDTime is a Twitch.tv channel and Discord community dedicated to 5th Edition Dungeons & Dragons (D&D). Our premier show, Pete & Jeremy's DnDTime, is a series of self-contained, one-shot adventures featuring our viewers! Head on over to Playing in DnDTime to learn more about the game, then sign up to play! We use Discord as our primary means of communication and Roll20 for all dice rolling and character management.


Our Content

We stream all sorts of D&D-related content throughout the week!

DnDTime Talks. A talkshow where Pete and Jeremy discuss all things D&D! Each show runs one hour and covers topics ranging from book reviews, to player and dungeon master advice. Wednesdays at 6:30 PM PT - Weekly.

Pete & Jeremy's DnDTime. Self-contained, one-shot adventures featuring our viewers! Each adventure runs no longer than one and a half hours, and there are three adventures each week. Adventures are completely self-contained and follow no particular storyline. Fridays at 6:00 PM PT - Weekly.

DnDTime Behind the Screen. Learn to prepare simple and engaging one-shot adventures using Roll20! Each show runs about two hours. Jeremy walks through converting 4th Edition Dungeon Delve adventures for 5th Edition D&D, and sets up the adventure in Roll20. Saturdays at 1:00 PM PT - Two shows per month see the weekly schedule on Discord.

DnDTime Delves. Good old-fashioned one-shot dungeon crawl adventures! Each adventure runs between three and four hours. Jeremy runs a cast of four characters through the adventures prepped in DnDTime Behind the Screen. Games are self-contained and quite deadly. Sundays at 6:00 PM PT - Two shows per month see the weekly schedule on Discord.

DnDTime Brews. Creating high quality homebrew for Pete & Jeremy's DnDTime! Each show runs between two and three hours. Pete and Jeremy hang out with the twitch chat and brew new character options, such as races, subclasses, or spells. Sundays at 6:00 PM PT - Two shows per month see the weekly schedule on Discord.
